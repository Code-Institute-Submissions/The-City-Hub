Latest from the Club

Update: COVID-19

The club have announced that they will begin the process to begin furloughing staff members who are unable to work during this current pandemic. Furloughing will safeguard future jobs.

Oxborough extends Contract

Goalkeeper Aston Oxborough has extended his contract until June 2022. He has spent the season on loan at National League South leaders, Wealdstone FC.

Fährmann cuts loan short

Ralf Fährmann has cut his loan spell short with the Canaries. He was ona season-long loan, but after not seeing much playing time, has decided to join SK Brann in Norway.

Transfers & Gossip

Aarons to Palace?

A lot of speculation has come from the Crystal Palace camp regarding Max Aarons. Max has been a regular in the first team for the past 2 seasons, and is grabbing the attention of the Premier League regulars.

Duda joins on Loan

Norwich City have acquired the uses on Ondrej Duda on loan until the rest of the Premier League season. Duda, and attacking midfielder joins from Hertha Berlin.

Rupp's a Canary

Lukas Rupp has joined Norwich City on a permanent deal until 2022. Rupp joins from 1899 Hoffenheim in the Bundesliga. The German is an attacking midfielder, comfortable playing on both flanks.
